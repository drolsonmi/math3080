* HOM - Homework: 0%-100%
* PRP - Project Proposal: Pass/Fail
* PRC - Project Checkup: 0%-100%
* PRF - Final Project: 0%-100%
* MID - Midterm Exam: 0%-100%
* FIN - Final Exam: 0%-100%
